Driftonaut

Internship project for EduGamiTec


Driftonaut is a 3D puzzle game that I developed in a team of 4 developers during a 12 week internship at EduGamiTec, an education technology start-up and developers of MemoMoti. In Driftonaut, the player takes control of an Astronaut that is stranded on some neatly placed asteroids and he needs to find his way back to his spaceship. To do so, he needs to solve a puzzle. On the grid-based map of the game, each tile has a behaviour, for example empty tiles slide the player forward, while walkable tiles allow the player to choose his direction. The player now needs to find the right path to reach the tile his spaceship is stranded on. Driftonaut is meant to be a showcase game for the MemoMoti platform, which we integrated into our puzzles.

Driftonaut was developed in Unity. I was working as a Programmer on this game.

Dwarf Miner

Internship project for EduGamiTec


Dwarf Miner is a 2D endless runner that I developed in a team of 4 developers during a 12 week internship at EduGamiTec, an education technology start-up and developers of MemoMoti. In Dwarf Miner, the player's goal is to safely lead a miner in his minecart through his mine. On his path, he regularly comes across deep gaps. To not fall down the gaps, the player needs to close them by building bridges with bridge parts. For each bridge part needed, the player gets asked a question, which needs to be answered by choosing the bridge part with the right answer to close the gap. The game also features power-ups and extra lives.

Dwarf Miner was developed in Unity. I was working as a Programmer on this game.

Rift Runners

An Arcade Machine built for a group project assignment (2nd semester)


Rift Runners is an Arcade Machine that I developed and built with my group for a 2nd semester group project. The game is 2D multiplayer competitive endless runner, inspired by the games SpeedRunners and Smash Bros. The objective of the game is simply to survive as long as possible, while trying to kick your opponents out of the game. It features many items that can be used to accomplish this, like ranged weapons that shoot projectiles (pistol, sniper, ...) or melee weapons (sword, hammer, ...). We also implemented some consumable items, that would have an effect on all the players, for example an item that would invert the controls. The Arcade machine itself was 2-sided and had 3 buttons and a joystick per player.

The game was developed in Unity and the Arcade itself was designed in Autodesk Fusion. I was mainly responsible for the multiplayer functionality, the character selection and the in-game UI. I also designed the Arcade machine.

Geography Party DELUXE

A group project assignment (1st semester)


Geography Party Deluxe was developed in a group of 5 people for the BTS-PowerWeek in the first semester of the curriculum. We were given 5 days to develop an educational game about luxembourgish geography. Additionally, we were given the task to create a short trailer and a flyer for our game We decided to make a 4-player Mario Party style game, with some interesting educational mini-games and 4 historical luxembourgish characters. Sadly, we only had time to implement one mini-game (a Trivia game) and the actual board. The game is missing a main menu and can only be played in 4-player mode, so it is more an early prototype than a finished product.

The game was developed in Unreal Engine. In this project, I was mainly responsible for the Trivia mini-game implementation.
